Kristina Alexandra

of House Vasa
Queen of Sverige

  • Born 18 December 1626
  • Died 19 April 1689
  • Reigned from 6 November 1632 until 6 June 1654
  • Very fond of books, manuscripts, paintings, and sculptures
  • Attracted many scientists to Stockholm
  • Interests included: religion, philosophy, math, and alchemy
  • Wanted Stockholm to become the "Athens of the North"
  • Met with Descartes in person
  • Gender non-conformist
  • Didn't want to marry, had "an insurmountable distaste for marriage"
  • Wore men's shoes out of convenience, slept 3 to 4 hours a night
  • Abdicated the throne in 1654 and converted to Roman Catholicism
